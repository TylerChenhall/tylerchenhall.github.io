About Me

I focus on creating custom training plans to help runners achieve their individual goals. In my personal running, I prioritize consistency, longevity, and enjoyment. I want to share the same mindset of healthy, injury-free, and enjoyable running experiences with the people I coach!

Coaching Services

I primarily offer custom training plans on a month-to-month basis. We'll

  • Chat about your running goals and history, either digitally or in-person
  • Create an updated training plan each month
  • Check-in and make adjustments to the plan on a weekly basis or as needed to make sure you're happy and healthy!
